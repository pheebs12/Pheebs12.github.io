Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup oil
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 cups flour
  • 3 tablespoons baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350
  2. In a medium bowl, cream together oil and sugar
  3. Sift together in a seperate bowl the flour, salt, and baking powder
  4. Add flour mixture to the oil and sugar, stir until the batter is smooth and well mixed
  5. Stir 2 well-mashed bananas into the batter
  6. Pour mixture into a loaf pan and cook for 45 mins
  7. Test for doneness by sticking a toothpick in the center of the bread. If clean, take the pan out of the oven and cool for 10 mins
